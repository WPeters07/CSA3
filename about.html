About SPX Power Washing

Our Story

SPX Power Washing began with two young entrepreneurs who wanted to make a difference in their hometown. What started as a small idea quickly grew into a community focused business built on hard work, honesty, and pride in every job we take on. We believe that great service doesn't have to come with big company prices, and we're committed to offering affordable, reliable work that helps keep our neighbourhoods clean and cared for.


Our Mission & Values


At SPX Power Washing, our mission is to provide reliable, high quality service at prices that make sense for the people in our community. We believe that small businesses can deliver big company results while staying honest, approachable, and committed to the neighbourhoods we grew up in. Our values guide every job we take on:

• Offering fair, affordable pricing without sacrificing quality


• Delivering results that match or exceed large competitors


• Treating every property with care and respect


• Giving back to our hometown whenever we can


• Staying honest, hardworking, and dependable on every job
